How It Works

Three layers of calm.

Real-time AI Scanning

Every post is scanned 1,500px ahead of your viewport using Llama Vision AI — before you even see it. Text and images are analyzed together for full context.

Multi-modal • Text + Vision

Traffic Light Indicators

A tiny dot appears on every post — green for safe, amber for emotionally heavy, red (pulsing) for graphic or intense content. At a glance, you decide what to engage with.

3-Tier Classification

Instant Trigger Protection

Intense posts are instantly covered with a frosted-glass blur overlay — complete with an AI-written reason. Click to reveal only when you choose to.

Blur • Reveal on click
Get Started

Up and running in 60 seconds.

No Chrome Web Store. No account. Just download and load — it's that simple.

01

Extract the .zip file

Download the extension and unzip it anywhere on your computer. You'll get a folder called vibe-check-extension.

02

Enable Developer Mode

Open Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions. Toggle on Developer mode in the top-right corner.

03

Load the extension

Click "Load unpacked" and select the extracted vibe-check-extension folder. Vibe Check is now active — start scrolling.
